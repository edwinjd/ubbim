Virtual Design Coordination

Optimizing Construction Projects with Advanced Digital Processes

Building Information Modeling (BIM): We use advanced BIM software to create detailed digital models of construction projects, allowing for more accurate cost estimates, improved project scheduling, and better collaboration among project stakeholders.

Virtual Reality (VR): Our team uses virtual reality technology to create immersive simulations of construction projects, providing clients with a virtual "walk-through" of the project before construction begins. This helps ensure that the project meets client expectations and reduces the risk of costly rework.

Data Analytics

Data-Driven Decision Making for Construction Projects

Aerial Analytics

We capture detailed images of construction sites from the air using advanced aerial platforms, and use software and tools to analyze this data for site analysis, progress tracking, and issue identification. Aerial analytics also allows us to create 3D models of construction sites for virtual design coordination and other purposes.

GHG Analytics

We measure, monitor, and report greenhouse gas emissions from construction projects throughout the project lifecycle, helping our clients to reduce their carbon footprint, meet sustainability goals, and comply with environmental regulations. By monitoring GHG emissions, we identify opportunities for emission reductions and make data-driven decisions to improve sustainability.
